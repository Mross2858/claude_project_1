Sales Decision Tree

Project Overview

The project follows philosophies outlined in Poke the Bear and Cold Calling Sucks and That's Why It's Important.

The goal was to stay on top of a talk track and ensure that when I received feedback during call reviews, I could codify it for future use.

It provided a way to standardize talk tracks, allowing for increased confidence in sales calls.

The idea was to integrate data analytics to A/B test the sequence and evaluate the performance of individual nodes.

The project had a limitation: it was difficult to implement the HTML script whenever I wanted to make changes, as it wasn't dynamic.

The node structure (e.g., 2YYN, 2YNY) was rigid and hard to monitor, which made data processing capabilities less effective.
